## 8. Photography Style Guidelines

### Reference Photo Styles

**1. Facility Photography:**
- **Lighting:** Soft, natural light
- **Angle:** Straight-on or slight angle
- **Composition:** Symmetrical, balanced
- **Color:** Warm tones, well-lit
- **Style:** Professional but inviting

**Examples to Follow:**
- Clean, organized ceremonial rooms
- Well-maintained exterior shots
- Product displays (coffins, urns) with dignity
- Staff photos (professional attire, warm expressions)

**Avoid:**
- Dark, shadowy photos
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Harsh flash photography
- Awkward angles

---

**2. Nature/Background Photography:**
- **Subjects:** Slovak mountains, forests, skies, flowers
- **Mood:** Peaceful, serene, hopeful
- **Time:** Golden hour (sunrise/sunset)
- **Weather:** Clear or soft clouds
- **Processing:** Slightly desaturated, warm tones

**Stock Photo Keywords:**
- "Peaceful mountain landscape"
- "Soft sunrise nature"
- "Tranquil forest path"
- "Gentle candle light"
- "White flowers close-up"

---

**3. Product Photography:**
- **Background:** Clean, neutral (white or beige)
- **Lighting:** Soft, diffused
- **Angle:** 3/4 view or straight-on
- **Focus:** Sharp on product
- **Context:** Tasteful staging (flowers, fabric)

---

## 9. Typography Combinations from Best Sites

### Combination 1: Classic Elegance (Recommended)

**Headings:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)  
**Body:** Inter (sans-serif)  
**Accent:** Playfair Display (serif)

**Why it works:**
- Timeless and dignified
- Excellent readability
- Professional yet approachable
- Free and widely supported

**Used by:** Many high-end funeral homes

**Headings:** Lora (serif)  
**Body:** Open Sans (sans-serif)  
**Accent:** Lora Italic

**Why it works:**
- Softer than traditional serifs

- Slightly more contemporary
- Free and performant

**Accent:** Libre Baskerville Italic

**Why it works:**
- Strong, confident headings
- Clean, modern body text
- Versatile and scalable

## 14. Accessibility Patterns from Best Sites

### High Contrast Mode
- Text: Minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio
- Large text (18pt+): Minimum 3:1
- Interactive elements: Clear focus states

### Keyboard Navigation
- Visible focus outline
- Logical tab order
- Skip navigation link
- All interactive elements reachable

### Screen Reader Optimization
- Semantic HTML (nav, main, article, aside)
- Alt text for all images
- ARIA labels for icon buttons
- Form labels properly associated

## 16. Competitive Analysis: Local Slovak Market

### Common Weaknesses in Slovak Funeral Home Websites:

1. **Outdated Design** (like current MIKO-RK site)
   - Built on old platforms (Webnode 2012-2015 era)
   - No mobile optimization
   - Poor typography
   - Flash elements or old JavaScript

2. **Poor Information Architecture**
   - Contact info hard to find
   - Services not clearly described
   - No pricing transparency
   - Missing emergency contact

3. **Weak SEO**
   - No meta descriptions
   - Poor keyword optimization
   - Slow loading times
   - No structured data

4. **Trust Issues**
   - No photos of facilities
   - No staff information
   - No testimonials or reviews
   - Outdated copyright dates

### MIKO-RK Opportunity:

By implementing modern design based on international best practices, MIKO-RK can:
- **Stand out** dramatically from local competition
- **Build trust** through professional appearance
- **Rank higher** in Google search
- **Convert more** visitors to clients
